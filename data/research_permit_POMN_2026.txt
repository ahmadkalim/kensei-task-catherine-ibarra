RESEARCH PERMIT — POMN KORNATI SURVEY 2026
Ministry of Environmental Protection and Energy, Republic of Croatia

Permit No: MEPE-2026-POMN-0842
Holder: Dr. Catherine Ibarra, University of the Adriatic Coast
Valid: January 1 — December 31, 2026

Authorized activities:
1. Underwater visual survey of Posidonia oceanica meadows
2. Collection of seagrass samples (leaf, root, rhizome) — max 50 shoots per site per season
3. Sediment coring — max 3 cores per site per season
4. Water column profiling (CTD, water samples)
5. Photographic documentation

Restricted activities (require addendum):
- Collection of protected species (Pinna nobilis, etc.)
- Use of robotic or autonomous underwater vehicles
- Sediment disturbance exceeding 0.5m depth

Reporting requirement: Annual activity report due by January 31, 2027.

Sites covered: Kornati Archipelago Marine Protected Area — all designated POMN monitoring stations (K-01 through K-14).
