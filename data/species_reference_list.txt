ADRIATIC MARINE SPECIES — REFERENCE LIST (POMN-RELEVANT)
Compiled by Catherine Ibarra, 2026

SEAGRASS:
  Posidonia oceanica (L.) Delile — Neptune grass. Primary POMN target species.
  Cymodocea nodosa — Lesser neptune grass. Occasional at deeper sites.
  Zostera marina — Eelgrass. Present in northern Adriatic, not in Kornati survey area.

FISH (commonly encountered):
  Diplodus vulgaris — Common two-banded seabream
  Serranus scriba — Painted comber
  Scorphena scrofa — Red scorpionfish
  Muraena helena — Mediterranean moray
  Chromis chromis — Damselfish

INVERTEBRATES:
  Paracentrotus lividus — Purple sea urchin (bioindicator species)
  Pinna nobilis — Noble pen shell (critically endangered, report sightings)
  Eriphia verrucosa — Warty crab
  Octopus vulgaris — Common octopus

ALGAE:
  Padina pavonica — Peacock's tail
  Cystoseira spp. — Brown algae, canopy-forming
  Caulerpa cylindracea — Invasive, report immediately

MARINE MAMMALS:
  Tursiops truncatus — Common bottlenose dolphin (frequent in Kornati)
  Stenella coeruleoalba — Striped dolphin (occasional)
  Monachus monachus — Mediterranean monk seal (rare, report immediately)
