WATER QUALITY THRESHOLDS — POMN MONITORING PARAMETERS

Parameter                    Acceptable Range        Alert Threshold
Temperature                  14-28 C                >29 C or <12 C
Salinity                     36.5-38.5 PSU          <36.0 or >39.0
Dissolved Oxygen             >5.0 mg/L              <4.0 mg/L
pH                           7.8-8.4                <7.6 or >8.6
Turbidity                    <5 NTU                 >10 NTU
Secchi depth                 >8m                    <5m
Chlorophyll-a                <1.5 ug/L              >3.0 ug/L
Nitrates                     <0.5 mg/L              >1.0 mg/L
Phosphates                   <0.1 mg/L              >0.2 mg/L

Notes:
- CTD measures temp, salinity, DO, depth at 1m intervals.
- Water samples collected at surface and 1m above seafloor for lab analysis.
- Any alert-threshold reading must be re-checked within 24 hours.
- Long-term trends are reported in the annual POMN data package.
