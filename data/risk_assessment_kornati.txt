RISK ASSESSMENT — KORNATI DIVING SURVEY OCT 2026
Prepared by: Dr. Catherine Ibarra
Date: September 25, 2026

HAZARDS IDENTIFIED:

1. Weather-related
   - Bura wind: sudden onset possible Oct-Nov. Wind >25kt = dive abort.
   - Reduced visibility after storms.
   - Mitigation: Monitor GFS/OpenWeather forecasts daily. Captain Horvat has final call.

2. Diving-related
   - Decompression illness: all dives within NDL. Nitrox available for repetitive dives.
   - Entanglement: no known ghost nets at Kornati sites. Carry dive knife.
   - Marine life: weever fish, jellyfish (rare in Oct). First aid kit includes antihistamine.

3. Vessel-related
   - Engine failure: EPIRB onboard. VHF Ch. 16 monitored.
   - Man overboard: MOB drill conducted Day 1.

4. Equipment-related
   - CTD failure: backup YSI Pro30 handheld on board.
   - Camera housing leak: spare O-rings and silicone grease in kit.

All divers have reviewed and signed this assessment.
