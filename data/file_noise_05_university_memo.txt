UNIVERSITY OF THE ADRIATIC COAST — INTERNAL MEMO

To: All Department of Marine Sciences Staff
From: Dean's Office
Date: October 1, 2026
Subject: End-of-Year Procurement Deadlines

All department heads are reminded that procurement requests for the 2026 budget year must be submitted by November 15, 2026. Requests received after this date will be processed against the 2027 budget.

The university procurement portal is open for submissions. Please ensure that all requests include:
1. A completed requisition form (available on the intranet)
2. Quote from the supplier (minimum one quote required for items under €5,000)
3. Budget code and remaining allocation
4. Approval from the relevant PI or department head for items over €350

For questions, contact the Dean's Office administrative team.
