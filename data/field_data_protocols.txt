POMN FIELD DATA PROTOCOLS — STANDARD OPERATING PROCEDURES
Version 4.2 (March 2026)

SITE SURVEY PROTOCOL:
1. Arrive at site coordinates. Drop anchor at least 50m up-current of transect start.
2. Deploy CTD to 1m below surface. Record temperature, salinity, DO, depth at 1m intervals.
3. Deploy Secchi disk. Record depth of disappearance and reappearance (average = Secchi depth).
4. Two divers enter water. Lay 50m transect tape along depth contour.
5. Record shoot density (number of shoots within 0.25m² quadrat) every 5m.
6. Collect leaf length samples (10 shoots) at 0m, 25m, and 50m.
7. Collect epiphyte scrape from 3 leaves per quadrat.
8. Collect sediment sample with hand corer at 25m mark.
9. Surface. Log data on POMN field sheet. Rinse all equipment with fresh water.

EQUIPMENT DECONTAMINATION:
- All gear rinsed with fresh water after each site.
- Wetsuits and dive gear soaked in mild disinfectant solution weekly.
- CTD rinsed with distilled water, dried, stored in padded case.

EMERGENCY PROCEDURES:
- Dive accident: surface immediately, administer O2, call Emergency Services (112).
- Lost diver: initiate search pattern, notify Harbour Master (Split Radio Ch. 16).
- Equipment failure: abort site, return to vessel, assess, contact Luka for backup.
